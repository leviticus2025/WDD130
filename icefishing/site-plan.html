Locations

We have a total of 3 local locations near Southeast Idaho for people to choose from! Information varies by each location so be sure to read the details of each one!
Palisades Reservoir
Located just 1 hr Southeast of Idaho Falls, Palisades Reservoir is a stunning scenic backgrounded reservoir with forest hillsides and about 70 miles of shoreline from which many consider a beautiful attraction that brings many anglers both during the winter and summer seasons.
Island Park Reservoir
Only 25 miles west of Yellowstone National Park and just shy of 2 hrs from Idaho Falls, Island Park Reservoir flows into Henry's Fork part of the Snake River and is a fan favorite for many types of entertainment, especially for fishing for unique types of fish during the winter months!
Mackay Reservoir
If you are looking for a place where the scenery never ceases to amaze, Mackay Reservoir is the place to be! Located 1 hr 30 min west of Idaho Falls, this high desert reservoir is full of kokanee fish and is stocked around the year with rainbow trout! Many adore the beastiful scenery that surrounds it!
